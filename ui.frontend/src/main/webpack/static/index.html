First Article Page

By Chirag Kumar Sharma

Bhagwan Parshuram Institute of Technology was established in 2007. The college offers profuse UG and PG level courses with multiple specializations. Admission to all the courses is done on the basis of the score obtained in the entrance exam followed by the counseling procedure. College accepts JEE mains for B.Tech., CET for BBA, and MBA.

Candidates who wish to take admission in the college have to register themselves online on the college official website and apply for the respective courses there. The highest package offered to the 2016-2020 batch was INR 30 LPA by Amazon SDE. Some of the top recruiters are Amazon, ZS Associates, Infosys, TCS, IBM, Airbus, Sopra Steria, HSBC, EPAM, IHS Markit, AIRTEL-X LABS, etc.
